Home

Welcome to the world of Shyam's music! Here, I share my passion for rap, from my inspirations to my favorite locations to record and more.

About Me

My name is Shyam, a 21-year-old hip-hop artist from Raleigh, NC. I love creating music that speaks to people from all walks of life.

My music journey began at a young age, starting with playing the Tabla (an Indian percussion instrument), and over time, I grew a passion for beats and transitioned into hip-hop.

Best Times to Create

I feel most inspired late at night when everything is calm, and I can focus completely on my music.

During these quiet hours, I feel more connected to my work, letting me share my truest thoughts.

Best Locations

Recording studios give me a great space to create. I also enjoy recording outside to find natural inspiration.

Some of my favorite places are home studios and outdoor spots where I can feel close to nature.

How I Make Music

I create music using digital audio tools and often write my lyrics on the spot.

My process blends the beats I make with lyrics that capture my thoughts and experiences.

Why I Make Music

Music is a meaningful way for me to share who I am, and I love connecting with others through my songs.

I believe music can open minds, and I hope my songs bring inspiration to those who listen.
