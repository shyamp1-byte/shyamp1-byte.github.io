Website Evaluations

Craigslist

Craigslist is an ads platform where users can post listings for various items and services.

  • Contrast: The white background with black and blue text doesn't stand out much, which may make it a little harder to navigate for some users.
  • Repetition: Adding more design elements could give the layout a more organized feel.
  • Alignment: There's some unused space, and a few parts feel a bit crowded, which can make it hard to focus on certain sections.
  • Proximity: Links and categories are placed quite closely, which may make it harder to tell different sections apart.

Overall, the site could benefit from improved CRAP principles to enhance its visual structure and user experience.

The New York Times

The New York Times provides news updates and articles on technology.

  • Contrast: The website uses clear contrast between text and background, making it easy to read articles.
  • Repetition: Repetitive use of the font and layout across the site helps users navigate smoothly.
  • Alignment: Text, images, etc. are well-organized, making the content easy to follow.
  • Proximity: Items like headlines and images are put together, making it easy for users to find what they need.

Overall, the New York Times uses the CRAP principles well, creating a clear, attractive, and easy-to-use website.
