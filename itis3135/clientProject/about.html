About Kriya Kitchen

Kriya Kitchen is a meaningful initiative of Hand in Hand USA, supported by a group of dedicated volunteers who are passionate about helping the community. Our main goal is to make a positive impact through providing healthy meals and offering outreach programs that support people in need.

Our Mission

At Kriya Kitchen, we believe that food has the power to bring people together and create real change. Our mission is to help communities by providing nutritious meals and organizing programs that improve the lives of those who need it the most. Whether we're serving meals at local shelters or hosting community events, we strive to make a lasting difference.

Our Team

Meet our team member, dedicated to serving the community with heart and compassion, bringing change one meal at a time.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We treat every person and community with kindness, care, and a desire to make a difference.
  • Sustainability: We focus on sustainable practices in our food sourcing and operations, so our efforts have a lasting and positive impact.
  • Collaboration: We work together with other organizations, local businesses, and volunteers to maximize our impact and help as many people as possible.
